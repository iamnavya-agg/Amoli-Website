Our Campaigns


Pass the Balloon

In November 2019, we launched our awareness challenge in which one had educate atleast one person about Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse. Then he/she had to paint a red balloon on the palm of the person educated and themselves and get a picture clicked. By uploading the picture on the social media handles with the hashtag #passtheballoon and nominating one of their friends to further spread the knowledge (Nominating here means passing on the balloon), a chain started to spread awareness . The red balloons represent the freedom and carefree attitude the children should be blessed with. And signifies the independence and innocence that the kids deserve.



Share your Story

We encourage everybody to speak up against any abusive incident of any form that you have experienced without any shame. Your one story can help a child out there to not suffer, but speak. We give you the platform to raise your voices and share your stories. We vow your confidentiality, until asked otherwise. Remember, it wasn’t your fault. You weren’t aware. But now, by sharing your story, you might safeguard the lives of a lot of children.


Speak up

Speaking up for those who cannot speak for themselves!
Experiences that kids have at a young age should be full of innocence and should not leave them with pain, anger, guilt or shame. So at Amoli we provide a safe and non judgmental platform to ‘speak up’ against Child abuse. We spread awareness about how rampant this violation against children is worldwide. We believe that we are not helpless and can make a difference in children's lives.
If we don’t speak up, then who will? After all, it shouldn’t hurt to be a child.






About Us

Amoli, meaning ‘precious’ is an NGO started by 8 college students, which educates children about the abnormality of Child Sexual Abuse prevailing in the society and helps in protection of their innocence.
