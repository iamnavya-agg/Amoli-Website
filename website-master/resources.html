BLOG

Amoli which started with 8 people has now extended to a large family of volunteers who have been actively working to provide help at the best. As the team grew so did the desire to reach people through different mediums. Our volunteers are managing and developing content that will help you understand CSA better and actively understand the current social scenario. These blogs have been curated to provide parents and caregivers the right set of information to help understand their children better and actively improve their social settings.

COMICS

Graphical representation has always aided learning better. Emerging research shows that comics and graphic strips are motivating, support struggling readers, enrich the skills of accomplished readers. About Us

Amoli, meaning ‘precious’ is an NGO started by 8 college students, which educates children about the abnormality of Child Sexual Abuse prevailing in the society and helps in protection of their innocence.
